Japan Commercial Arbitration Journal

This September, we are delighted to announce the publication of the inaugural issue of Japan Commercial Arbitration Journal. The mission of the journal is to foster a wider understanding of arbitration and ADR in Japanese context.

We gratefully acknowledge the efforts of all contributing authors, the editorial board members, and our supporting law firms.

This inaugural issue discusses the law and practice concerning dispute resolutions in Japan. On top of that, it covers the JCAAâ€™s new arbitrations rules and introduces new ADR facilities. It is also significant for foreign lawyers to note the amendment of Act on Special Measures concerning the Handling of Legal Services by Foreign Lawyers under which the restrictions on their practice have been largely eased.
